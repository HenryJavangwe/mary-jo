Our Services

Introspective hypnosis

During introspective hypnosis the client takes an inward journey to explore their subconscious mind in order to find the origins of psychosomatic symptoms.

our conscious mind tends to 'protect us' by forgetting difficult or traumatic events in our lives, due to the fact that these memories are repressed, over time it may manifest as a symptom in our bodies, serving as a trigger causing health related issues.

It can also cause problems on an emotional and mental level affecting our relationships,work, finances etc. This can keep us stuck preventing us from reaching our highest potential and can also keep us from experiencing joy, love, health, happiness, abundance and authenticity.

Forgiveness therapy

Unforgiveness towards others or towards yourself only hurts you and keeps you stuck. Forgiveness therapy provides clients with a higher perspective and understanding regarding past related hurts.

It helps clients release unresolved baggage that if not dealt with can cause feelings of resentment, anger and bitterness towards self or others which ends up having a negative impact on their life.

Role change

During hypnosis clients are able to access others (living or deceased) by using their mind, because we are all consciousness and connected.

Role change gives clients the opportunity to communicate with the other person and establish a dialogue to get the other person's point of view, two sides of the story is obtained and this allows a better understanding of the other person's actions. Forgiveness can be elaborated and a higher perspective is gained.

Past life regression

The soul is eternal and incarnates into different physical forms through time to learn about polarity while remembering it's divine nature.

We as souls have had many experiences (past lives) to learn lessons, evolve and expand. Sometimes the cause of a present issue affecting our life now can originate from a past life. Past life regression helps the client find the origin of the issue and once found and understood from a higher perspective healing can take place and we are no longer affected by it.

Spirit Releasement

During the process of dying there are a number of factors that influence the way the spirit detaches from it's body.

Some will easily cross the threshold, others will remain on this plane attached to their possessions, their loved ones,their belief system, and even their thirst for revenge; and a few will not realize they have passed on and will remain disoriented amongst us.

After the death of the physical body;those who remain here become lost souls, keeping their fears,traumas,judgements, and convictions. It is they who for different reasons attach to our vibratory field, consciously or unconsciously, transmitting their energy and symptoms to us and making us perceive them as our own.
